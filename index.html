Download .zip SHU Beta App ↗
01
Get the SHU Beta App
  • 1. Download SHU Beta from utility-beta.cfw.sh and install it on your Android phone.
  • 2. Germany only — connect a VPN to any country outside Germany before opening the app. If it still does not load, go to Settings → Apps → ScooterHackingUtility → Force Stop, make sure the VPN is active, then open the app again.
02
Flash the Firmware
  • 3. Open SHU Beta and tap Connect to pair with your scooter via Bluetooth.
  • 4. Navigate to the Flash tab at the bottom of the app.
  • 5. Tap Flash from File, then select the .zip file you downloaded above.
  • 6. Tap Flash and keep your phone nearby. Do not close the app or lock your screen until it finishes.
  • 7. Once complete, power off the scooter fully. The firmware is now installed.
⚠ Disconnecting mid-flash can leave the scooter unresponsive. Keep your phone close and the screen on until the process is done.
Speed control
Unlock & Reset
The speed unlock is session-only — it resets every time you turn the scooter off.
Unlock Max Speed
Hold the Right Brake and the Accelerator simultaneously while powering the scooter on.
Keep holding until error codes appear on the display — that confirms the speed cap has been bypassed.
You need to repeat this every ride. The unlock does not persist after shutdown.
Reset to 22 km/h
Turn off the scooter as normal.
The next startup without the unlock method will automatically cap speed at 22 km/h.
